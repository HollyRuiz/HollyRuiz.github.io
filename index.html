The Beginning

Posted May 20, 2019

I started looking up how to create a portfolio for my artwork and heard about an upcoming Code101 class through Codefellows that would teach me how to build a website in a day! So, I went and I worked with a team and we created a website about Seattle Artwalks. They also talked about their courses and recommended Codeacademy and Khanacademy to learn the Intro to HTML, CSS and Javascript Tutorials, which also would be a good prep for their entrance exam at Codefellows.

Right Here, Right Now...

Posted May 20, 2019

Well after taking the Codeacademy Intro to HTML and part of the CSS course the 7 day free trial of pro access ran out, which leads me right here so I decided to check out Khanacademy. To be honest, I think Code Academy has a nicer learning program but they charge you to get everything. I have learned quite a bit of HTLM5 and really love the design possibilities of CSS. I am enjoying what I'm learning so far and putting that knowledge to use while I work on creating an amazing portfolio! I could even see myself changing careers! What else is possible?
